About Us

Welcome to Acellabe Game, a creative studio with a singular focus: crafting delightful and accessible casual games for everyone. We believe that games should be a source of joy, a relaxing escape, and a wonderful way to brighten your day. Our mission is to build charming worlds and intuitive gameplay that feel instantly familiar and endlessly fun.

As a small, passionate team, we pour our hearts into every detail, from the art style to the satisfying feel of a perfect move. We are dedicated to creating high-quality experiences that respect our players' time and bring a smile to their faces.

Our Games

Project Bloom (Working Title)

Genre: Relaxing Puzzle & Decoration
Platform: Mobile (iOS & Android)
Status: In Development

Our debut title invites you into a serene world waiting to be brought to life. In "Project Bloom," you'll solve vibrant color-match puzzles to earn stars, which you can then use to restore and decorate your own magical garden. It’s the perfect blend of satisfying challenge and creative expression, designed for moments of calm and delight. We can't wait to share more with you soon!
